Definition: Dark chocolate is a type of chocolate that contains a high percentage of cocoa solids and cocoa butter, with little to no milk content.

Key Benefits:

  • Rich in Antioxidants: Contains flavonoids that help protect against cell damage.
  • Heart Health: May reduce blood pressure and improve circulation.
  • Brain Booster: Enhances cognitive function and reduces stress.
  • Improves Mood: Stimulates endorphin production for a better mood.

Nutritional Facts (Per 100g):

  • Calories: 600 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 43g
  • Protein: 7g
  • Fiber: 10g
